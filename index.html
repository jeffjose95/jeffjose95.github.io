About Me

To me, coding is like art. Anyone can paint but, what differentiates a novice from a true artist is the quality of their work and innovation. There is nothing more enjoyable for me than working on an interesting project and trying to make it a reality.

I am always striving to learn new material and don't enjoy staying stagnant. I joined the game development club at my college because it gave me the opportunity to test my skills and to innovate beyond class curriculum. I quickly learned how to use a few game engines and have worked on several personal projects since then. Presently, game development is a hobby that I pursue in my spare time.

My other hobby is film development because it gives me the chance to meet and work with people whom I wouldn't normally meet on a daily basis. Every year, I attend a competition hosted by CampusMovieFest which is a college level competition for making a 5 minute short film in 1 week. I was able to win the Jury award in 2016 which is an award given to the top 4 films of a college.
